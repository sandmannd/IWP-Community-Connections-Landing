Frequently Asked Questions

Are these official IWP events?

No. Community Connections adventures are organized by members and are not facilitated, monitored, or organized by IWP staff.

How do I join an adventure?

Open the adventure, review the details, and use the Register button when registration is required.

Can I attend only part of a multi-day adventure?

Yes, when the organizer allows day visitors or partial attendance. The registration page will show the available attendance choices.

Where do I get help?

For Invisible Wounds Project support and services, visit Get Help.
