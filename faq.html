Frequently Asked Questions

Are these official IWP events?

No. Community Connections adventures are member-organized and are not facilitated, monitored, or organized by IWP staff.

Who can participate?

Each adventure lists its own eligibility, age, family, capacity, and registration details.

How do I register?

Open an adventure and select Register. Follow the steps shown for that specific event.

Where can I get help?

For IWP support resources, visit the IWP Get Help page.
